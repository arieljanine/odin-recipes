Instant Pot Turkey Chili with Beans

Description

This quick and easy Instant Pot Chili Recipe is loaded with ground beef, beans and tomatoes in a hearty and spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground turkey
  • 1 15 oz can pinto beans, drained
  • 1 15 oz can black beans, drained
  • 1 15 oz can kidney beans drained
  • 1 15 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 3 tbsps tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 jalapenos, diced
  • 2 tbsps chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp oion powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Set the Instant Pot to SAUTE mode and add ground turkey. Brown and crumble the turkey until no longer pink. Drain any excess fat from the turkey and return liner to Instant Pot.
  2. Add in remaining ingredients and give it a stir.
  3. Attach lid, making sure valve is set to sealed position. Turn to Bean/Chili Setting or HIGH pressure for 18 minutes. Once finished cooking, release pressure and enjoy!
